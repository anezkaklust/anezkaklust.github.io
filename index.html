Anežka Klustová

About

I am a PhD student in Particle Physics at Imperial College London interested in neutrino-nucleus interaction cross-sections and neutrino oscillation. In 2020, I graduated with MSci Natural Sciences degree from University College London, majoring in Particle Physics with a minor in Inorganic and Materials Chemistry.

Research Postgraduate & Neutrino Physicist.

Member of the MINERvA collaboration and the DUNE collaboration.

  • City: London, UK
  • Email: a.klustova20@imperial.ac.uk

On MINERvA, I work on the measurement of nuclear dependence in antineutrino scattering. On DUNE, I am the lead geometry, simulation, and reconstruction developer for the near detector gaseous argon prototype in the test beam at Fermilab. On the hardware side, I participate in the commissioning, assembly, testing, and operation of the detector.
